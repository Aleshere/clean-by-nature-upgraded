Vacation Rentals Cleaning Services

Tourism might be down because of the pandemic, but believe it or not there are still people travelling around. We offer vacation rentals cleaning services, providing that peace of mind of a clean space for both the owner and the traveller. Our range of services goes from AirBnb cleaning, to occasional after visits cleaning, to deep cleaning for a property you just decided to rent out. Example of services include dusting the surfaces, shaking out rugs and mats, replacing sheets and linens, cleaning windows or doors. And of course bathroom sanitization.
